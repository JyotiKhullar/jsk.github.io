Editing for Clarity and Structure

Context: Product description for ProductX Studio (anonymized for demonstration)

Problem:

  • Long, dense sentences made the content hard to read
  • Repetition of key ideas (no-code, customization)
  • Unclear flow between concepts
  • Mixed focus between technical and non-technical audiences

What I improved:

  • Broke complex sentences into shorter, clearer statements
  • Removed redundancy and tightened phrasing
  • Reorganized content for logical flow (what → how → why → technical detail)
  • Clarified technical concepts like base packages and schemas
  • Balanced readability for both technical and non-technical users

Before:

ProductX Studio, the core of the ProductX product suite, is a no-code platform to automate workflows and build applications while enjoying the maximum degree of freedom. Built on a composable no-code architecture, the platform empowers non-technical individuals and no-code creators to effortlessly design and customize their distinctive business solutions using pre-built, composable components. The platform is required for any ProductX instance. The ProductX platform functionality is implemented in base packages. You can customize out-of-the-box functionality. The customizations extend functionality by replacing the base schemas and do not modify base packages and their schemas.

After:

ProductX Studio is a highly customizable no-code platform that helps you automate workflows and build applications. Its no-code architecture empowers non-technical individuals to design and customize their business solutions using pre-built components. The studio sits at the core of the ProductX product suite, which also means it is required for every ProductX instance. The platform lets you customize out-of-the-box functionality by extending its base packages. That is, you can customize the platform features by replacing the base schemas, without modifying the base packages or their schemas.

Outcome:

  • Improved readability and scannability
  • Clear separation of concepts for better understanding
  • Better alignment with both user and technical audiences
  • Content is easier to reuse, structure, and adapt for AI-based retrieval
